Blinkit: Recipe-to-Cart
Bridging content & commerce in quick grocery delivery
🛒

Problem

Users browsing recipes on YouTube/Instagram manually translate them into 8-12 individual Blinkit searches. An untapped "planned cooking" segment sits right below the surface with 2B+ monthly food views on YouTube India and zero integration with grocery.

AOV opportunity
5-7 items → 12-18 per recipe order
Top RICE score

Feature Design

North Star

Recipe-originated orders as % of total orders. Target: 5% in 3 months, 1.8x AOV vs regular orders.

Inshorts: Quality-First Feed
Replacing engagement-based ranking with a News Quality Score
📰

Problem

The feed optimises for swipe volume - amplifying clickbait, creating echo chambers, and eroding notification trust by blending ads with editorial content.

Solution: News Quality Score

Multi-dimensional ranking: Source Credibility (25%) + Editorial Rating (25%) + Factual Density (20%) + Uniqueness (15%) + Timeliness (15%). Feed: 40% Must-Know · 35% Personalised Quality · 15% Serendipity · 10% Hyperlocal.

3-Tier Notifications

Breaking (NQS >950, max 2/day, never ads) · Daily Digest (timed to optimal windows) · Weekly Roundup (re-engagement).

Growth & AI Strategy
Khan Academy: Closing the Engagement Gap
EdTech, Khanmigo AI adoption & international B2B expansion
🎓

Problem

Only 11% of KAD-licensed students qualify as Very Active Learners. Khanmigo wasn't proactively sought - students lacked the metacognition to ask for help. Engagement design lags Duolingo by years.

Key Insight

Khanmigo's pivot: embed AI directly in the practice flow rather than offering a standalone chatbot. Reducing activation energy beat improving the feature itself - a lesson that generalises to any product.

Three Recommendations

Streak-based goals (DAU/MAU 15% → 25%) · Proactive AI nudges (Khanmigo 5% → 30%) · KAD Lite international (YVAL 1.1M → 5M in 3 years)

Feature Addition · 0-to-1
Playo: Squad Mode
Solving the biggest retention gap - no one consistent to play with
🏸

Problem

Playo's loop is transactional - every session is a fresh matchmaking event coordinated on WhatsApp, outside the app. Users who want a consistent group have no native mechanism to form one.

Squad Mode

Named group of 3-8 members with recurring slot booking, weekly RSVP check-ins, and a Smart Bench of 4 fill-in players. When someone taps "Out," the bench is auto-alerted and the spot filled - no WhatsApp scrambling. Attendance Reliability Scores add social accountability.

North Star
Sessions/user/month: ~4 → 7+
30-day retention
38% → 58%+ for squad users

Monetisation & Engagement
Duolingo: The Streak Economy
How habit loops power a $7B EdTech flywheel
🦉

The Core Loop

Duolingo's 27% DAU/MAU is built on the streak - a daily commitment device powered by loss aversion, not intrinsic motivation. You open the app to protect the streak, not necessarily to learn Spanish.

Tensions

The Hearts system punishes mistakes - antithetical to education but effective at driving upgrades. Low correlation between streak length and actual proficiency is a long-term brand risk as AI tutoring matures.

What PM-me would do

Introduce a "Proficiency Streak" - tracks consecutive days above the user's difficulty floor. Harder to game, better correlated with outcomes, differentiable vs. competitors.
