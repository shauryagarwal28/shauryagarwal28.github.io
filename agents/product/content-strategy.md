## Tone Guidelines

- **Professional but human** — not corporate jargon, not overly casual.
- **Confident, not arrogant** — "I led" and "I built" not "I helped with" or "I was involved in."
- **Specific over vague** — "200+ employees" not "many employees"; "30%" not "significant reduction."
- **PM vocabulary** — use terms naturally: user discovery, go-to-market, prioritisation, KPIs, north star metric.
