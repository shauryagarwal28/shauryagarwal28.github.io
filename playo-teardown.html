01 - About the Product

What Playo does today

Playo's core loop is transactional: discover a venue, book a slot, play. The matchmaking feature helps users find opponents or teammates for single sessions. What's missing is a persistent social layer - a way for users who want to play regularly with the same group of trusted people to form and manage that group within Playo. Today this coordination happens entirely on WhatsApp, outside the app.

02 - Problem Statement

The consistency gap

The biggest friction for regular sports players isn't finding a venue. It's finding consistent people to play with.

"I love Playo for booking courts but every session I'm on WhatsApp for an hour trying to confirm who's coming, who dropped out, and whether we need a fill-in. Playo has nothing for this."- Badminton player, 29, Bangalore

Users who play 3+ times per week have the highest LTV potential but also the highest churn risk - their engagement with Playo is limited to the booking step. The group formation and coordination loop (which is where the real habit lives) happens on WhatsApp. This means Playo doesn't own the retention habit. It only owns the transaction.

07 - Solutions Aligned with Business Goals

What to build and why

Squad Creation

Named group of 3-8 core members with sport, skill level, preferred day and time, and preferred venue. Created in 3 taps. Invite via code or deep link.

Goal: bring coordination inside Playo
Weekly RSVP

Automatic notification 3 days before each session: "Are you in?" One tap to confirm or decline. Session status visible to all members in real-time.

Goal: replace WhatsApp confirmation chaos
Smart Bench

4-person on-call pool per squad. When a core member taps "Out," bench is instantly notified with full session details. First to accept fills the spot.

Goal: eliminate last-minute dropout risk
Attendance Reliability Score

Visible score per member based on confirmed-vs-attended ratio. Displayed next to name in squad view. High scorers eligible for Squad Karma multiplier.

Goal: social accountability, reduce dropouts
Recurring Slot Booking

Squad admin sets a recurring slot (every Wednesday 7 PM). Playo auto-holds the slot and sends a 3-day advance RSVP. Reduces weekly rebooking friction.

Goal: increase booking frequency, GMV
Squad Karma System

Points for showing up, filling in for bench slots (1.5x multiplier), and high attendance streaks. Unlocks perks: priority booking, discount on 10th session.

Goal: retention and habit reinforcement

10 - Go-to-Market Strategy

How we launch and grow

Phase 1
Week 1-4
Closed beta with power users

Recruit 500 power users (5+ sessions/month) in Bangalore and Hyderabad. Give them early access to Squad Mode. Collect qualitative feedback on RSVP friction, bench flow, and reliability scores. Fix core UX issues before public launch.

Phase 2
Week 5-8
Public launch with social proof

Roll out to all cities. Feature "Squad Stories" in the app - squads that have been playing together for 3+ months. Create a referral mechanic: forming a squad earns the creator 1 free court booking. Target: 10,000 squads formed in first month.

Phase 3
Week 9-16
Squad tournaments and Karma perks

Launch squad-vs-squad casual tournaments for badminton and football - structured competition that drives session frequency. Introduce Karma perk: squads with 90%+ average reliability get priority booking access for premium weekend slots.

North Star: Sessions per active user per month. Squad users target 7+ vs 4 for non-squad. This drives both GMV (more bookings) and retention (social accountability).

Guardrail: Fill-in acceptance rate must stay above 60%. If bench players regularly decline, the trust signal breaks and the feature loses its value proposition.
