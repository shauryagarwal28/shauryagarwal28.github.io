Problem

Only 11% of KAD-licensed students qualify as Very Active Learners. Khanmigo wasn't proactively sought - students lacked the metacognition to ask for help. Engagement design lags Duolingo by years.

Company Teardown
Supercell: Games People Play for Years
How a 300-person studio from Helsinki built a $10B+ empire - and stayed relevant for 15 years

The core thesis

Most mobile games are designed for installs. Supercell looked at this model and identified it as a product design failure. Their founding bet: build games deep enough to play for years, and the economics follow. Clash of Clans proved it - 68% of players came via word of mouth, not paid ads.

Three retention loops that stack

Every Supercell game is built on layered loops. The daily habit (resource timers force return 4x/day). The social obligation (missing a Clan War attack feels like letting teammates down - no notification replicates this). The progression milestone (there is always a next Town Hall in sight, months away). Remove any one loop and retention collapses at that stage.

The philosophy that built the empire

Cell teams of 10-17 with full creative autonomy. No greenlight process from management. 30+ games killed before players ever saw them. "Would you be devastated if this shut down?" is the only filter that matters. The less management had to do with the game, the better its chances.

How they adapted

Three eras: the build phase (2010-2016, €2.1B peak, Tencent acquires for $8.6B), the plateau (2016-2022, no new hits, small teams became an obstacle for live games), and the reinvention (2023-present, split into New Games vs Live Games, CoC team doubled, revenue rebounds strongly in 2024). The strategy that gets you to the top is not the strategy that keeps you there.

Feature Addition · 0-to-1
Playo: Squad Mode
Solving the biggest retention gap - no one consistent to play with

Problem

Playo's loop is transactional - every session is a fresh matchmaking event coordinated on WhatsApp, outside the app. Users who want a consistent group have no native mechanism to form one.

Squad Mode

Named group of 3-8 members with recurring slot booking, weekly RSVP check-ins, and a Smart Bench of 4 fill-in players. When someone taps "Out," the bench is auto-alerted and the spot filled - no WhatsApp scrambling. Attendance Reliability Scores add social accountability.

North Star
Sessions/user/month: ~4 → 7+
30-day retention
38% → 58%+ for squad users

Monetisation & Engagement
Duolingo: The Streak Economy
How habit loops power a $7B EdTech flywheel

The Core Loop

Duolingo's 27% DAU/MAU is built on the streak - a daily commitment device powered by loss aversion, not intrinsic motivation. You open the app to protect the streak, not necessarily to learn Spanish.

Tensions

The Hearts system punishes mistakes - antithetical to education but effective at driving upgrades. Low correlation between streak length and actual proficiency is a long-term brand risk as AI tutoring matures.

What PM-me would do

Introduce a "Proficiency Streak" - tracks consecutive days above the user's difficulty floor. Harder to game, better correlated with outcomes, differentiable vs. competitors.
