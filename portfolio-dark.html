Blinkit: Recipe-to-Cart
Bridging content & commerce in quick grocery delivery
🛒

Problem

Users browsing recipes on YouTube/Instagram manually translate them into 8–12 individual Blinkit searches — an untapped "planned cooking" segment with 2B+ monthly food views on YouTube India and zero grocery integration.

AOV opportunity

Feature Design

North Star

Recipe-originated orders as % of total orders. Target: 5% in 3 months, 1.8x AOV vs regular orders.

Khan Academy: Closing the Engagement Gap
EdTech, Khanmigo AI adoption & international B2B expansion
🎓

Problem

Only 11% of KAD-licensed students qualify as Very Active Learners. Khanmigo wasn't proactively sought — students lacked the metacognition to ask for help. Engagement design lags Duolingo by years.

Key Insight

Khanmigo's pivot: embed AI directly in the practice flow. Reducing activation energy beat improving the feature itself — a lesson that generalises to any product.

Three Recommendations

Streak-based goals (DAU/MAU 15% → 25%) · Proactive AI nudges (Khanmigo 5% → 30%) · KAD Lite international (YVAL 1.1M → 5M in 3 years)

Monetisation & Engagement
Duolingo: The Streak Economy
How habit loops power a $7B EdTech flywheel
🦉

The Core Loop

Duolingo's 27% DAU/MAU is built on the streak — a commitment device powered by loss aversion. You open the app to protect the streak, not necessarily to learn.

Tensions

The Hearts system punishes mistakes — antithetical to education but effective at driving upgrades. Low correlation between streak length and actual proficiency is a long-term brand risk.

What PM-me would do

Introduce a "Proficiency Streak" — tracks consecutive days above the user's difficulty floor. Harder to game, better correlated with outcomes, differentiable vs. competitors.
